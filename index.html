Hello Kitty - Original

Hello Kitty is a globally recognized character created by the Japanese company Sanrio. She is depicted as a white cartoon cat with a red bow on her head. Hello Kitty is known for her simple and cute design, which has made her popular among people of all ages.

Despite being portrayed as a cat, Hello Kitty has a distinctive human-like appearance with her lack of mouth adding to her charm.

Hello Kitty - Cupid

Cupid Hello Kitty is a special edition of the popular character Hello Kitty, often released around Valentine's Day. In this version, Hello Kitty is depicted as a cherubic cupid, wearing a diaper, angel wings, and a bow and arrow. The character retains its signature simplicity and cuteness while incorporating symbols of love and romance.

Cupid Hello Kitty is often featured in merchandise such as plush toys, stationery, and themed decorations.
